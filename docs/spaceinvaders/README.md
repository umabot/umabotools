# Space Invaders

A browser-based recreation of the classic Space Invaders arcade game, built entirely with HTML5, CSS, and JavaScript.

## Description

Space Invaders is a timeless arcade classic originally released in 1978. This single-page web application brings the retro gaming experience to your browser with no installation required. Battle against waves of alien invaders as you defend Earth from extraterrestrial threats!

## How to Play

### Objective
Destroy all the alien invaders before they reach the bottom of the screen. Survive as many waves as possible and achieve the highest score!

### Controls

- **Arrow Keys (← →)** or **A/D Keys**: Move your ship left and right
- **Spacebar**: Fire your laser cannon
- **Mobile**: Touch controls for movement and shooting (if implemented)

### Gameplay

1. **Your Ship**: You control the green ship at the bottom of the screen
2. **Aliens**: Rows of alien invaders move horizontally across the screen and gradually descend
3. **Shooting**: Fire lasers to destroy aliens before they reach you
4. **Enemy Fire**: Watch out! Aliens shoot back
5. **Shields**: Use barriers for protection (if available)
6. **Lives**: You typically start with 3 lives
7. **Scoring**: Different alien types award different points

### Tips

- **Aim for the Top Row**: Higher aliens are usually worth more points
- **Use Cover**: Hide behind shields when enemy fire gets intense
- **Corner Aliens**: Take out aliens on the edges—they make the group move faster as their numbers decrease
- **Stay Mobile**: Keep moving to avoid enemy fire
- **Don't Rush**: Accuracy is more important than speed

## Features

- Classic arcade gameplay
- Responsive controls
- Score tracking
- Multiple alien types
- Progressive difficulty
- Retro sound effects (if enabled)
