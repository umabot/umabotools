# No Silver Bullets in Data and AI, but Process360 Can Help Deliver Value Fast
## Abstract
> [!Abstract] lots of new and very similar tools, databases, etls, modelling, etc, and new ways to do things, including new jargon (that means something similar: data warehouse, data lake, data lakehouse, swamp, operational data store), makes it more critical to plan accordingly your data architecture, without losing the objective that data is for doing something, and have to bring value to the business. How can Process 360 can help you?

BigData & AI Paris was a blast! I had a great time learning and networking with other data enthusiasts at the Palais des Congrès (Porte Maillot) for the last two days. After talking and participating in several ateliers, the abundance of innovative approaches to capturing, managing, and operationalising company data left me pondering the question: are we all just a little overwhelmed?

If you are old, you know we have been wrestling with data since... the time of the first clay tablets maybe.

And if you are young and new to the field my advice: there are no silver bullets.

💡Data Mesh, a lateral approach to optimize collaboration between business users and IT for speedy data app delivery, is captivating. However, let's not lose sight of our end users' expectations—value is what drives them.

Alternatively, the choice between on-premise, Cloud, or Hybrid models for data utilization may be weighing on your mind. But amidst this decision-making process, it is crucial to strike a balance between costs, performance, and scalability. Are you keeping a watchful eye on your infrastructure TCO? 🎯

AI anyone? I imagine you mean LLMs, but do not forget about the old and good linear regression analysis. Are you inclined towards frequentist or Bayesian ideologies? What matters is providing value.

In a world where focusing on value is paramount, especially for end users relying on Pega and Salesforce, Process360 stands as a specialist. We understand the intricacies and complexities, ensuring tangible results swiftly. There are no silver bullets—just a dedication to delivering value. Contact Process360, and let's drive value together.
